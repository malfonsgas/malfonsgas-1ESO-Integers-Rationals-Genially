Youth hostels are an invaluable resource to backpackers, and enable young people and students to travel from place to place without blowing that all important budget in the first few days. Cheap, basic, friendly and safe, they are the perfect place to meet fellow travellers and bed down for the night.

We want to book accommodation in one youth hostel in Biarritz and we need to know the number of rooms we will need.

There are 48 girls and 42 boys. To organize rooms we have to consider that:

  1. We need to book the least number of rooms.
  2. There must be the same number of people in each room.
  3. Girls and boys are not mixed.
